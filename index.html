🌞 Our Amazing Solar System 🌍

Let’s take a fun journey through space and meet the planets!

☿ Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet. It's closest to the Sun and very hot!

♀ Venus

Venus is super bright! It’s hot and has thick clouds all around it.

🌍 Earth

Earth is our home! It has water, air, animals, and YOU!

♂ Mars

Mars is red and dusty. Scientists think people might visit it someday!

♃ Jupiter

Jupiter is HUGE! It has a big red spot, which is a giant storm!

♄ Saturn

Saturn is famous for its rings. They look like hula hoops!

♅ Uranus

Uranus is blue and chilly. It spins on its side like a rolling ball!

♆ Neptune

Neptune is far, far away. It’s windy and has deep blue colors!
